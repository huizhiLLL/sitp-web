About the Platform

The Fuel Cell Catalyst Platform is a comprehensive research tool designed to support fuel cell catalyst development through data management and AI-powered performance prediction.

Using the Prediction Tool

  1. Select your catalyst type from the dropdown menu
  2. Adjust the metal content using the slider
  3. Choose the support material
  4. Enter optional parameters like surface area and particle size
  5. Set operating conditions (temperature and humidity)
  6. Click "Predict Performance" to see results

Understanding Results

  • Mass Activity: Activity per unit mass of catalyst (A/mgPt)
  • Specific Activity: Activity per unit surface area (mA/cm²)
  • ECSA: Electrochemical surface area (m²/g)
  • Stability: Performance retention after cycling (%)

Note: This is a demonstration platform. Predictions are simulated for educational purposes and should not be used for actual research decisions.
